About Jeevamrut

Jeevamrut is a natural liquid fertilizer and traditional organic manure. It is an excellent source of natural carbon, biomass, nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and huge lots of other essential micro-nutrients required for your crops.


Benefits

  • Acts as an agent to assume cellular development.
  • Increases the microbial count in the soil significantly.
  • Helpful in faster decomposition of organic matter in the soil.
  • Improves the immunity of the plant system.
  • Enhances earthworm activity in the soil.

Preparation Method

Mix cow dung, cow urine, jaggery (brown sugar), pulse flour, and soil from the farm bund in water. Ferment this mixture for 48 hours in the shade, stirring twice daily. It is then ready for application.
