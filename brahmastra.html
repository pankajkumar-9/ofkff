About Brahmastra

Brahmastra is a powerful organic pesticide used to control larger insects and larvae that are difficult to manage with milder repellents. It is typically used when pest infestation is high.


Benefits

  • Controls all kinds of worms, borers, and caterpillars.
  • Acts as a broad-spectrum pesticide without the side effects of chemicals.
  • Extremely potent yet biodegradable.

Preparation Method

It involves boiling crushed neem leaves, custard apple leaves, papaya leaves, pomegranate leaves, and guava leaves in cow urine. The mixture is boiled until reduced by half, then kept for 24 hours. It is filtered and stored for use.
