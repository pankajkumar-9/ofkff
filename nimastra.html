About Nimastra

Nimastra is a botanical pesticide prepared principally from neem leaves. It is highly effective against sucking pests and mealybugs. Unlike chemical pesticides, it does not harm beneficial insects or pollinators.


Benefits

  • Effectively controls sucking pests and whiteflies.
  • Safe for the environment and non-toxic to humans.
  • Cost-effective as it uses locally available natural resources.
  • No residual toxicity in the final produce.

Preparation Method

Crush neem leaves and mix them in water with cow urine and fresh cow dung. Let the solution ferment for 48 hours, stirring occasionally. Filter and spray directly on crops.
